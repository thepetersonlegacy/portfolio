# Creating Your Lead Magnet PDF Guide

## File Name Required
`10-website-mistakes-guide.pdf`

## Location
Place the PDF file in the `public/` directory of your project.

## Recommended Content Structure

### Cover Page
- Title: "10 Website Mistakes Costing You Clients"
- Subtitle: "A Free Guide by Eldon Peterson"
- Your logo/branding
- Professional design with your brand colors

### Introduction (Page 1)
- Brief introduction about why this matters
- Statistics about website conversion rates
- What readers will learn

### The 10 Mistakes (Pages 2-12)

1. **Slow Loading Times**
   - Why it matters: 53% of users abandon sites that take >3 seconds to load
   - How to fix: Image optimization, CDN, code minification

2. **Poor Mobile Experience**
   - Why it matters: 60% of traffic is mobile
   - How to fix: Responsive design, mobile-first approach

3. **Weak or Missing Call-to-Actions**
   - Why it matters: Users need clear direction
   - How to fix: Strategic CTA placement, compelling copy

4. **Complicated Contact Forms**
   - Why it matters: Each field reduces conversions by 10%
   - How to fix: Minimize fields, use smart defaults

5. **Lack of Social Proof**
   - Why it matters: 92% of consumers trust recommendations
   - How to fix: Add testimonials, case studies, trust badges

6. **Unclear Value Proposition**
   - Why it matters: Users decide in 3 seconds if they'll stay
   - How to fix: Clear headline, specific benefits

7. **Poor Navigation**
   - Why it matters: Users leave if they can't find what they need
   - How to fix: Simple menu structure, search functionality

8. **Missing Trust Signals**
   - Why it matters: Security concerns kill conversions
   - How to fix: SSL certificate, privacy policy, security badges

9. **Weak Visual Hierarchy**
   - Why it matters: Users scan, they don't read
   - How to fix: Proper headings, whitespace, contrast

10. **No Clear Next Steps**
    - Why it matters: Confused visitors don't convert
    - How to fix: Guide users through a clear journey

### Conclusion (Page 13)
- Summary of key takeaways
- Call-to-action to schedule a consultation
- Your contact information

### About the Author (Page 14)
- Your bio and credentials
- Your services overview
- Contact information and social links

## Design Tips

- **Use your brand colors** (gray-900, white, accent colors)
- **Professional fonts** (similar to your website)
- **High-quality images** or illustrations
- **Consistent formatting** throughout
- **White space** for readability
- **Page numbers** and branding on each page

## Tools to Create the PDF

### Option 1: Canva (Recommended for Non-Designers)
1. Go to canva.com
2. Search for "Lead Magnet" or "eBook" templates
3. Customize with your content
4. Download as PDF

### Option 2: Google Docs/Slides
1. Create content in Google Docs or Slides
2. Design with your branding
3. File > Download > PDF

### Option 3: Adobe InDesign (Professional)
1. Professional layout tool
2. Full design control
3. Export as PDF

### Option 4: Hire a Designer
- Fiverr: $50-$200
- Upwork: $100-$500
- 99designs: $300-$1000

## File Specifications

- **Format:** PDF
- **Size:** Keep under 5MB for fast downloads
- **Pages:** 10-15 pages recommended
- **Dimensions:** 8.5" x 11" (US Letter) or A4
- **Resolution:** 150 DPI (web-optimized)

## After Creating the PDF

1. Save as `10-website-mistakes-guide.pdf`
2. Place in `public/` directory
3. Test the download by clicking the CTA on your website
4. Verify the PDF opens correctly on desktop and mobile
